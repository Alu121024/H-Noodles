Info

On a clean surface, mix the whole wheat flour and almond flour (or oat flour). Create a well in the center and add the eggs, salt, and olive oil. Beat the eggs and gradually mix with the flour from the edges. If the dough is too dry, add water one tablespoon at a time until you achieve a homogeneous dough. Knead the dough for about 10-15 minutes until it is smooth and elastic. Form a ball and cover it with a damp cloth. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.

Recipe

How to make the pasta

While the dough is resting, chop carrots, peas, and celery into small pieces. After the resting period, roll out the dough and cut it into the desired shapes. In a separate pot, cook the chopped vegetables until they are slightly tender. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water for about 3-5 minutes, or until al dente. Combine the cooked pasta with the cooked vegetables.
